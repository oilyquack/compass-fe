## 1. What Information Do We Collect?

### Personal information you disclose to us

**In Short:** We collect personal information that you provide to us.

Personal information may include:

- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Debit/credit card numbers
- Billing addresses
- Contact or authentication data
- Contact preferences
- Names

**Sensitive Information:** We do not process sensitive information.

**Payment Data:** If you make purchases, payment information is handled by **Skiddle** ([privacy policy](https://www.skiddle.com/terms/privacy-policy/)).

All personal information provided must be true, complete, and accurate.

### Information automatically collected

**In Short:** Some data such as IP address, device type, and browser characteristics is collected automatically.

Examples include:

- Log and usage data (timestamps, pages viewed, errors, etc.)
- Device data (hardware model, OS, carrier, etc.)
- Location data (GPS or IP-based location, depending on device settings)

### Information collected from other sources

We may also collect information from public databases, marketing partners, affiliates, social media, and other providers.
